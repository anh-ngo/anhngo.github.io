About us

Ngo in Northern Vietnamese means corns. We are the Ngo siblings and both have myopia. This was our simplified logic for naming this site Myopic Corns. Perhaps this shouldn’t have surprised anyone!

I’m Han Ngo

Hi there. I’m Hannie Ngo.

I’m Anh Ngo

I am an engineer on the medical journey and will be entering medicine as a doctor. I was having a hard time with the analytical reading section called CARS in the MCAT exam. Getting daily reading has helped to improve my reading skills and the CARS score. As a side effect, the urge to write comes along with the more I read as a way to store ideas and further dive into my thoughts.

I am a healthcare worker , a chembio engineer , a learner , an occasional ceramist , and ultimately, a family member .

House Rules

We are growing into two cool old ladies spending time reading and writing, especially instead of sleeping when travelling on planes. This site is established for us to constructively mark down our subjective opinions and experiences.
